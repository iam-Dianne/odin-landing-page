CHARACTERS

Garnet

Garnet is the fusion of Ruby and Sapphire and the current de-facto leader of the Crystal Gems.

Amethyst

Amethyst is the last known Gem manufactured on Earth as a part of the Gem Homeworld's Kindergarten project, being one of the last surviving Gems on Earth.

Pearl

Pearl was Pink Diamond's second Pearl, and one of Rose Quartz's closest followers and her sole confidant.

Steven

Steven Quartz Universe is the titular main protagonist of the Steven Universe franchise. He is the son of Greg Universe and Rose Quartz, the only known hybrid of a human and a Gem and the first Crystal Gem of human descent.
